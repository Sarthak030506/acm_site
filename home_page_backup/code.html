Welcome to ACM NMIET

We are a vibrant community dedicated to advancing computing as a science and profession. Our chapter serves as a hub for creativity and technical excellence, providing students with unique opportunities to grow, learn, and connect with industry leaders and like-minded peers.

Through workshops, hackathons, and guest lectures, we bridge the gap between academic theory and real-world application. Whether you are just starting your journey in technology or looking to master advanced concepts, ACM NMIET is your platform to excel.
