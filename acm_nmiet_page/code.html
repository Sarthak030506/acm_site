Welcome to ACM NMIET

Established in 2025, the ACM NMIET Student Chapter is dedicated to fostering technical growth and student excellence through innovation and collaboration.

We provide a platform for students to explore the latest in computing, connect with industry experts, and build projects that matter. Our community thrives on the curiosity of our members and the drive to solve real-world problems.
