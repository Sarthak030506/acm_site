Frequently Asked Questions

How long does membership last? expand_more
ACM membership is valid for one year from the date of purchase. You will receive a renewal reminder before your membership expires.
Can I upgrade my membership later? expand_more
Yes, students can upgrade to Professional membership upon graduation. You can manage your membership type within the myACM portal.
Is this different from the local chapter fee? expand_more
Yes, Global ACM membership is separate from local chapter dues. However, being a global member often waives certain local fees and provides significantly more resources.
